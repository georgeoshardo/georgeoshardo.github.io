  Arrangements

Supervisions are weekly. Please submit your worksheets to me via email or Teams 48 hours before the supervision. You can drop them off to me in my pigeonhole at Wolfson College, or you can send them to me by scanning them (e.g using an app like Office Lens).
I will return the sheets to you with worked solutions before the supervision and we can go through some of the things you are struggling with.
Supervisions will happen in: Wolfson College, Pembroke College, or Mill Lane Lecture Theatres.
